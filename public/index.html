How Float works

Drag, drop, or tap Add

Desktop users can drop files into the bubble. On mobile, Add opens the upload sheet.

Paste text straight in

Paste copied text anywhere in the room and Float turns it into a drifting text asset.

Text copies, files download

Tap a pasted text asset to copy it to the clipboard. Tap a file asset to download it.

Settings shape the room

Lifetime mode and expiry

Keep assets until you delete them, remove them after the first grab, or expire them on a timer.

Theme and share link

Switch the room theme, copy the share URL, and scan the QR code from the settings sheet.

Clear bubble when needed

Room controls also let you clear every floating asset at once after re-entering the password.
